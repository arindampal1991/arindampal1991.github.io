Air Fryer Recipe

Toast and Omelette

8 minutes in Air Fryer, Ingredients: Bread, Egg, Oil, S & P

by Arindam Pal on July 21, 2020

To fry eggs, you need to preheat the Air-Fryer. One smart way to do it is to toast the bread in them.

Step 1: Place the breads vertically in the Air Fryer. Like so

A different view

Step 2 : Air Fry them for 2 mins at 150 degree celcius. This process will preheat the fryer for eggs.

Step 3: Take the bread toasts out.

 

Step 4:

Remove the Air Fryer basket carefully (because it’s still hot) from the pan. You might see breadcrumbs in the pan. Throw them away.

Since you don’t have the handle of the basket now, you need to use your thumbs with the hole in the pan to hold and move it. Like so

Closer view:

Step 5: Once taken out, the pan needs to stay straight. So, put it on something to keep it horizontal.

Step 6: Put two teaspoons of cooking oil in the hot pan. Crack an egg in it.

Step 7: Put the pan back inside and turn the Air Fryer on for 4 minutes, 200 degree celsius.

Ta da! Take it out with a spatula. And look closely.

Tip:

If sometimes your egg fluffs up too much, or remains uncooked, use a spatula to flip it and cook the other side for 2-4 minutes at 200 degree celsius. Like so

This is the opposite side after trying the second innings.

Add with the bread already toasted. Your breakfast is ready.

Bon Appétit!
